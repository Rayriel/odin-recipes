Tomatoes

Description

Tomatoes are a versatile fruit that can be used in a variety of dishes. They are rich in vitamins and antioxidants, making them a healthy addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the tomatoes thoroughly.
  2. Slice the tomatoes into desired thickness.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve fresh or use in your favorite recipes.
